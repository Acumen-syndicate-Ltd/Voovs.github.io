Audio suggestions

  • Pieces- Calm and controlled song with a good beat. Apple used this one for one of their commericals
  • All Night- A sort of very upbeat rap. Very good to get hyped up!!! Used in Apple's animoji commercial
  • Pull It- A sort of rock song, with only one guitar solo throughout the whole song. Apple used this one for the IMac Pro ad
  • Dance For Me Walls- More classical than anything, though it does have cinematic traits
  • Free Bird- A song that really gets you pumped up. Used in many of the greatest action scenes, including the church scene in Kingsmen. I'd suggest skipping to 4 minutes
  • Renegade- This one is really good for workouts. Short, loud, and overall nice
  • Feel It Still- Has a pretty nice beat in general. I'm rather sure that this is a more modren song than many of the others
  • Run- Fast paced song that was in one of Apple's Macbook Pro ads
  • No- It's that music that you hear in vlogs. No words, just a pretty basic beat. The song makes good ambient sounds
  • The Thieving Magpie- A very well known classical song. One of the best sound tracks you could use for building up magnitude. BBC used this one in that Moriarty crown jewels scene. That is if you're familiar with Sherlock
  • Make It Look Easy- Pretty nice upbeat song. Used in the Google Pixel 2 commercial
  • Always Look On The Bright Side Of Life- From Monty Python's Life Of Brian. A bit of an optimistic song, coming from a bunch of guys getting crucified
  • William Tell Overtune- You won't recognise the name, though you will recognise the sound! This one was heard in the award winning Macbook Pro "Bulbs" commercial
  • Back Pocket- It's got a sort of upbeat, though not overpowering, tune. Found in Apple's Apple Pay commercial
  • Take On Me- Very cinematic and great live music
  • It's A Hard Knock Life- You'd think a bunch of children singing in unison would shatter the glass; then you hear this. There's also a newer version, with better sound quality though it compensates on singing ability
  • Hey, Soul Sister- The comment section is equally surprised about this being from 2009. A nice upbeat song, with vocals
  • Humanism- Sound so jazzy and upbeat, dancing is irresistible. Used to be the theme song for The Late Show with Stephen Colbert
  • Stuck In The Middle With You- An upbeat feeling retro style song. Found in a SovietWomble video
  • Shut Up And Drive- A standard dance-inducing song. Ironically, found in the Apple Carpool Karaoke commercial
  • New Soul- Astonishingly uplifting song. Hand picked by the legendary Steve Jobs for the Macbook Air commercial
